Adde Bedfa3 — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Adde Bedfa3 is a free personal finance tracker app developed by CedarForge.

Data We Collect

Adde Bedfa3 does not collect or transmit any personal data. All data you enter (income, expenses, savings) is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to any server.

Ads

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device information and usage data to show relevant ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more details.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our app. This data is anonymous and used only to improve the app.

Children

This app is not directed at children under 13.
